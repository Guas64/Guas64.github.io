by: Nicholas Miller and Ryder Griffin


List of needed items:
(Click on the name to see the price and image)


  1. Motherboard
  2. CPU (Centeral Processing Unit)
  3. RAM (Random Access Memory)
  4. GPU/video card (Graphical Processing Unit)
  5. Modem card (Optional)

Step 1: Putting the CPU in the motherboard


Unhook the cpu clamp by pushing on the circled lever and pushing to the side.


Next lift up the metal flap on the cpu.


Now take the CPU and line up the notches with the ones on the board (DO NOT FORCE THE CPU IN THE SLOT).

Now after you get the cpu in close it back up the same way you opened.



Step 2: adding the fan and RAM


take the fan and line the screw holes around the CPU on the motherboard and screw it in.

Take the RAM line up the slot in the RAM slot and push in untill you hear 2 clicks

Should look like this

Step 3: install the GPU/Video card and the Modem card

Take the GPU/Video card and press it into the long white slot until it clicks. (Circled in red)

Take the Modem card and press it into the shorter white slot until it clicks. (circled in red)
